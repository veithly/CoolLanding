— chapter 03 / pipeline

Six passes, one frame.

  1. 01

    GPGPU simulation

    Curl-noise advects every particle inside a floating-point FBO, ping-ponged each frame.

    pos₁ = sim(pos₀, curl, attractor, shock)
  2. 02

    HDR points

    Positions are drawn as additive points through a real perspective camera into a half-float scene buffer.

    blendFunc(ONE, ONE)
  3. 03

    Bright-pass

    Only highlights above threshold survive into the bloom chain — the rest goes dark.

    if (luma < 0.62) discard
  4. 04

    Separable blur ×3

    A ping-ponged Gaussian with a growing radius builds soft, wide bloom cheaply.

    blur(H) → blur(V), r += 1
  5. 05

    Filmic composite

    Scene + bloom are tone-mapped, then chromatic aberration shifts the channels.

    tonemap(scene + bloom·k)
  6. 06

    Grain & vignette

    A final film grain and vignette glue the frame together — never a flat gradient.

    + grain(uv, t) · vignette
